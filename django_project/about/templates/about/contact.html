About

Video game reviews + me

What is Vgre.me?

Vgre.me is a game review website that gives you my perspective on video games. It's not my job to review video games nor do I make any money from this website. All reviews I post are my personal opinion, and if you disagree let me know :)

Who Am I?

My name is Ali Waseem. I grew up in Calgary, Alberta, Canada and playing video games is my hobby. I studied at the University of Calgary as an Electrical Engineering with a Minor in Computer Engineering and I love to program and tinker with technology.

Made with <3

Vgre.me is created with the popular web framework Django, Bootstrap and Iron Summit Media's Clean blog template. Check out the Git repositories for Django and Clean Blog for your next project!
